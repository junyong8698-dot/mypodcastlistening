## Reddit — r/algotrading

**Title:** I lost 40% on emotional BTC trades, so I built an autonomous AI agent to trade for me. Here's what happened.

**Body:**

Last year I got liquidated at 4AM on a leveraged BTC position. Classic retail story. I decided to remove myself from the loop entirely and build an AI-powered trading system.

The stack:
- **OpenClaw** (open-source AI agent framework) running on a Mac Mini
- **Claude** as the LLM brain
- **Telegram** as the control interface
- **Binance** for execution

The system runs a regime detection model (bull/bear/neutral) using macro data from FRED, on-chain metrics, and technical indicators. It has an autonomous R&D agent that runs ~22 experiments per day testing new strategies.

The wildest part: I built a 9-member AI board of directors that votes weekly on strategy changes. Each board member has a different risk profile and expertise area. The R&D agent proposes changes, the board debates and votes, and winning proposals auto-deploy.

I've been writing up the whole process as a series. Part 1 covers the origin story and why I went fully autonomous:
